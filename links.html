LINKS

中国 CHINA

China-EMU (中国动车组) - China (including Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan) - all Multiple Units from China, as its name suggests.
站如其名，涵盖所有国内动车组。

Trainnets (非人狂想屋) - China (including Hong Kong, Macau and Taiwan) and sometimes overseas - train archives of China (only in Simplified Chinese).
国内火车资料库。

Narrow Gauge Railways in China (你好，小火车！) - China Mainland - Narrow Gauge Railway archives of China (only in Japanese).
大陆地方工矿窄轨资料库；站长为日本人，因此使用日语。

Rick's Rail/Fly Travelogue (记·忆·1435MM) - China Mainland, Hong Kong and Overseas - blog run by Rick from Hong Kong, featuring their own travel and transport experience.
香港人 Rick 的个人博客，记载其香港、内地与海外铁路/航空摄影旅程。

STEAM IN CHINA　中国大陸を駆けた蒸気機関車たち - China Mainland - Run by Yangrou from Japan, featuring last steam locomotives and corresponding railway lines in China.
日本人“羊肉”的个人网站（日语），记载中国大陆最后留存的蒸汽机车及线路。“西河煤矿”一节离本站长家乡非常近。

日本 JAPAN

Naeboworks (苗穂工房) - Japan (Hokkaido) - abandoned railways in Hokkaido (and other regions of Japan). Feature: Hokkaido Train Timetable on 1 Mar 1967 .
北海道及日本其他地区废线资料库。有一张1967年3月1日版的北海道列车时刻表 。

Train Front View (駅旅·ゆけむり研究室) - Japan and overseas - miscellaneous railway stuff, including hand-drawn train icons (Japan, China, and other countries), sightseeing trains, hot spring, and high-speed trains.
铁路相关杂项，包括两万多个手绘列车图标（日本、中国及其他国家），观光列车，温泉，高速列车等。

Tempoku (天北工房) - Japan (Hokkaido) - abandoned railways in Hokkaido, especially Tempoku Line. Some purchased content such as historical videos and games are hard to obtain outside of Japan.
北海道废线资料库，重点为天北线。部分内容须购买，但由于为实体光盘，在日本之外很难获取。

Hokkaikankobushi (北海観光節) - Japan (Hokkaido) - railway, culture, and travel information of Hokkaido.
北海道铁路、文化及观光信息。

欧洲 EUROPE

Langs de Rails - Netherlands - Run by a Dutch named Nico Spilt, featuring European railway photography.
荷兰人 Nico Spilt 的个人网站，专注欧洲铁路摄影轶事。

Railfan Europe - Pan-Europe - excellent database for rolling stocks from Europe.
极好的欧洲火车资料库。

RailUK Forums - United Kingdom and World - UK Railway Forums, literally.
站如其名，英国铁路论坛。

Bring the night trains back on track - Europe - Night train promotion website, featuring a night train map of Europe.
夜行列车提倡活动网站，有一张欧洲夜行列车线路图。

vagonWEB - Europe - The information archive of the consists and timetables of trains of Europe, mainly Central Europe.
欧洲列车编成及时刻表信息库，主要为中欧。

美洲 AMERICA

Steam Locomotive - America and World - information and archives of steam locomotives in North America and around the world.
世界蒸汽机车资料库，重点为北美。

Rail Pictures - America and World - pictures of trains of North America and around the world, literally.
站如其名，世界列车照片库，主要为北美。

SteamLocomotive.info - America and World - pictures of steam locomotives of North America and around the world, literally.
站如其名，世界蒸汽机车照片库，主要为北美。
